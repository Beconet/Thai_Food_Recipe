Creamy Omelet Recipe

- material

3 chicken eggs

50 grams of ham

Spring onion 2 grams

Fresh butter (salted) 1 tablespoon

1 teaspoon soy sauce

black pepper

cooked rice

- method

1.Cut the ham into bite-sized pieces and chop the spring onions and set aside.

2.Put the cracked eggs in a mixing bowl, season with soy sauce and beat until the eggs are well mixed.

3.Heat the pan, Add oil to cover, then add fresh butter, ham and beaten eggs.

4.Stir together by dragging them back and forth.

5.When the level of doneness that you want is reached, then turn off the stove.

6.Scoop the thickened eggs onto a plate.

7.On hot steamed rice, sprinkle with sliced spring onions.

8.ground pepper, ready to serve
