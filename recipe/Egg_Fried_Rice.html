Crispy Pork with Holy Basil

Egg Fried Rice

-ingredient

minced garlic

vegetable oil

2 duck eggs

cooked steamed rice

Sauce

soy sauce

granulated sugar

sliced spring onion

black pepper

spring onion

lemon

- method

1. Heat a pan and add vegetable oil. When hot, add garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add eggs and mash until cooked.

2. Add rice and mix well. Season with seasoning sauce, soy sauce and sugar.

3. Add spring onions and stir until combined. Turn off the heat. Transfer to a plate, sprinkle with pepper. Serve with spring onions, lime and fish sauce chili.
