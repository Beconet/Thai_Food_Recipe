Crispy Pork with Holy Basil

-ingredient

1 cup of steamed rice

Sliced crispy pork as desired

10 cloves of Thai garlic

4 red chili peppers

1 red chili, sliced diagonally

2 tablespoons seasoning sauce

1 tablespoon dark soy sauce

2 tbsp fish sauce

2 tablespoons of sugar

2 tablespoons of water

- method

1.Prepare a mortar and pestle. Chop Thai garlic and red chili peppers, add them and pound them until crushed.

2.Heat a wok, add oil, add garlic and pepper, stir fry until fragrant.

3.Add crispy pork and season with seasoning sauce, dark soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and water. Stir until the ingredients are combined.

4.Add basil leaves, followed by diagonally sliced chilies. Stir and mix together.

5.Finish serving on a plate.
